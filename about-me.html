Motivation

Can you see the hidden digit in the middle plate? Yes! Then you can pursue in whatever career you will choose.

No!! ☺ Give me a hug! You are diagnosed with color-blindness. Search google immediately for your career help!

I started clicking at the age of 17 and I fell in love with images. At that time, I thought the statement was just a philosophical quote but later I realized Jean-Luc was right. My life changed during the first year of my college when I came to know that I am color blind (red-green) and this was the time when I discovered the soul the pixel. Even if I cannot see some digits in Ishihara plate, I can visualize them from the pixels. I made up my mind to do something for the color blind people ever since and shortly, came across Image Processing. I made a program which can help color blind students to identify digits from Ishihara plate. Gradually, I started loving Engineering, Image processing as well as Machine learning.

Now can you see the digit? This is the beauty of Image processing! For me it was HUGE! I could see what I couldn't. Imagine a blind person seeing his/her daughter for the first time! This took only two lines of Matlab's code
